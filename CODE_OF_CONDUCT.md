# Code of Conduct

We follow a zero-harassment policy and expect respectful, professional collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

1. Be respectful and constructive.
2. Discuss ideas, not people.
3. Accept feedback in good faith.

## Unacceptable Behavior

1. Harassment, discrimination, hate speech, or personal attacks.
2. Sexualized language/content or threats.
3. Doxxing or publishing private information without permission.

## Reporting

1. Report via private channel described in `SECURITY.md`.
2. Do not disclose sensitive reports in public issues.

Maintainers may remove/edit content or restrict participation for violations.
